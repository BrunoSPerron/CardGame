The Json filenames in this folder are used as locations_id.
 i.e. this is how they are referenced when you need them.

Note: Once your mod is published, your files nay be referenced by
	other mods. It's proper etiquette not to rename your files and
	keep them in the mod even if you're no longer using them, for
	compatibility purposes.
	(It doesn't affect anything in-game anyway)
